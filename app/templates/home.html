About Malwareintelvault

MalwareIntelVault was founded during Summer 2017 to bring together a tight-knit group of malware researchers to combine efforts and collaboratively share information with the awesome malware hunting community.

The team uses a common tracking platform to collaboratively record observables, perform research and catalogue data with a view to releasing research findings through the group's public blog.

Why?

Because MIV members believe in the imperative of giving back to the open source community which time and time again has proven invaluable in the course of their careers.
